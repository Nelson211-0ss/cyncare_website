About our CEO

Dr. Kayla Nicole Jdiobe

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Dr. Kayla Nicole Jdiobe brings an exceptional blend of education and hands-on healthcare experience to CynCare Health Staffing. With a bachelor's degrees in nursing and a bachelor's degrees in marketing/human resources, along with a Master and Doctorate in Healthcare Administration, she understands healthcare from every angle.

Her journey from CNA and CMA to healthcare recruiter spans 15 years, giving her unique insight into both patient care and staffing needs. This comprehensive perspective inspired the founding of CynCare Health Staffing, where she combines clinical expertise with strategic workforce management to deliver innovative staffing solutions.
